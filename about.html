Introduction

In this study, we examined the rental prices and house prices of these regions with the data taken from 2 different districts of Ankara (Ümitköy and Bahçelievler) and İzmir (Denizbostanlısı and Nergiz).

We created two different econometric models. Our first model is for rental prices of houses.

The dependent variable of this model is log(Fiyat), and the explanatory variables are Bulunduğu_kat , Eşyalı , m², Oda and sehir. Descriptions of the explanatory variables are given below.

Bulundugu_kat:: shows which floor the flat is on.

Esyali:: is a dummy variable. If the house is rented furnished, it takes the value of 1 if it is rented unfurnished then, it takes value of 0.

m²:: indicates the size of the house in terms of square meters.

Oda:: shows how many rooms the house has. There are only 2 types of houses in the dataset; Houses which have 3 rooms or 4 rooms.

Sehir:: is a dummy variable. It takes the value of 1 for rental houses in Ankara and it takes value of 0 for those located in Izmir.

Our second model was created for houses for sale in the same region. The dependent variable of this model is log(Fiyat), and the explanatory variables are Bulunduğu_kat , Bina , m², Oda and sehir. Descriptions of the explanatory variables are given below.

Bulundugu_kat: shows which floor the flat is on.

Bina: is a dummy variable. If the building age is 15 or younger, the building is defined as young; If the building is older than 15 years, the building is defined as old. Then, young buildings take the value of 1 and old buildings take the value of 0.

m²: indicates the size of the house in terms of square meters.

Oda: shows how many rooms the house has. There are only 2 types of houses in the dataset: Houses which have 3 rooms or 4 rooms.

Sehir: is a dummy variable. It takes the value of 1 for rental houses in Ankara and it takes value of 0 for those located in Izmir.

With the two different models we created, we aimed to see to what extent the variables affect the rental prices and house prices for 2 different districts in Ankara and Izmir.
